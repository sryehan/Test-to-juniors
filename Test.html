MEMS Photoacoustic CO₂ Sensor — Test Bench

Longitudinal resonator cell driven by a DFB laser at 4.23 µm, read out via WMS‑2f/1f lock‑in detection on the driver PCB in frame. Benchtop assembled on an optical breadboard for characterization sweeps from 5 to 5000 ppm CO₂.

Laser
DFB, 4.23 µm (also VCSEL @ 2.004 µm)
Detection
WMS‑2f/1f, Zurich Instruments MFLI
Sensitivity
~0.466 µV/ppm, R² = 0.997
LOD
~16 ppm CO₂
Part of an Erasmus Mundus WAVES MSc thesis on MEMS‑based photoacoustic gas sensing. Modeling in COMSOL Multiphysics; lock‑in processing in MATLAB. sryehan.com
